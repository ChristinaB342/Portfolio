My Background

My name is Christina Brown and I'm an aspiring graphic designer, illustrator and graphics designer.
Producing and communicating in an effective and artistic way has always facinated me as it
challenges me to think outside of the box and to also unleash the creativity inside of me.


This portfolio is a way of getting to know Christina more from a professional standpoint to a personal
one in order to land a position in a working environment by the end of my school years.
I have always been interested in the arts and design, and I have pursued this passion through my
education and personal projects. I am currently studying graphic design at university, where I am
learning the skills and techniques needed to create effective and engaging visual communication.
